# Where to Buy Digital Comics

Looking for great digital comics to read in Panels? Here are the best places to purchase or download DRM-free comics you can import directly into the app.

## Panels Store — Buy Direct, Support Creators

[![Panels Store — DRM-free digital comics marketplace](/img/store-banner.png)](https://panels.store)

**[Panels Store](https://panels.store)** is our own DRM-free digital comics marketplace, built specifically for Panels readers.

Here's why it's the best place to buy:

- **Instant access** — purchases sync directly to your Panels app
- **DRM-free** — you own your comics, forever
- **Creator-first** — 80% of every sale goes directly to the creator
- **No exclusivity** — creators can sell everywhere, we just make it easy
- **100,000+ active readers** have already discovered titles on the store

Browse new releases, bestsellers, and free titles at **[panels.store](https://panels.store)**.

## Other DRM-Free Comic Stores

We also recommend these trusted retailers for DRM-free comics you can download and import into Panels:

- [Humble Bundle](https://www.humblebundle.com/books?partner=panels) — bundles of comics at pay-what-you-want prices, often supporting charity
- [Panel Syndicate](http://panelsyndicate.com/) — pay-what-you-want comics by top creators like Brian K. Vaughan
- [DriveThruComics](https://www.drivethrucomics.com/) — huge catalog of indie and genre comics
- [Abstract Studio Comics](https://abstractstudiocomics.com/shop/) — home of Strangers in Paradise and other Terry Moore titles
- [3 Worlds / 3 Moons](https://3w3m.substack.com/) — curated indie titles
- [Bad Ink Studios](https://badinkstudios.com/shop/) — indie creator comics
- [GenSeven Comics](https://gen7comics.com/) — indie publisher
- [Blue Fox Comics](https://bluefoxcomics.com/collections/digital) — digital comics storefront
- [eManga](https://emanga.com) — manga and global comics in DRM-free formats

## Free & Legal Comics

These sites offer free, Creative Commons, or public domain comics you can legally download and read in Panels:

- [Pepper&Carrot](https://www.peppercarrot.com/) — award-winning open-source webcomic by David Revoy
- [Comic Book Plus](https://comicbookplus.com/?cid=1507) — thousands of public domain comic books
- [Digital Comic Museum](https://digitalcomicmuseum.com) — pre-1959 public domain comics archive
- [NASA Moonikin Comic](https://www.nasa.gov/moonikin-comic/) — official NASA comic about the Artemis mission
- [NASA First Woman](https://www.nasa.gov/calliefirst/) — NASA's graphic novel series about the first woman on the Moon
- [Overwatch Media](https://overwatch.blizzard.com/en-us/media/stories/) — free official Overwatch lore comics
