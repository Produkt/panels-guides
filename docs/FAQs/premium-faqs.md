# Purchases and Subscriptions

### What is the difference between Panels+ and Panels 3?

**Panels+** is a subscription that unlocks all features for as long as the subscription is active. We offer trial periods for both monthly and yearly plans.  

**Panels 3 (4, 5, 6, …)** is a one-time purchase. It unlocks all current features included in that major version. For example, if you purchase Panels 3, you get access to **all** premium features in Panels 3, including any that are added later. When Panels 4 is released with new features, you’ll still keep everything from Panels 3, but new features introduced in Panels 4 will require a new purchase.  


### If I buy or subscribe to Panels on one device, can I also use it on another? Do I need to pay again?

No, you don’t need to pay again. As long as you’re signed in with the same Apple ID, you can go to **Settings** and tap **Restore Purchases**. Even if you attempt to re-purchase Premium with the same Apple ID, you won’t be charged twice.  


### Can I share Panels purchases with my family members?

Yes. All of our purchases support **Family Sharing**.  


### I removed Panels and lost my Premium access. How can I get it back? Do I need to pay again?

You don’t need to pay again. On the purchase screen, tap **Restore Purchases**.  
If you run into any issues, please contact us at **support@panels.app**.  


### Is it a good time to purchase the current major version, or should I wait?

We don’t have a fixed release schedule. Panels 4 will most likely be released sometime in **2026**.  


### Will I receive a discount on the next major version if I purchase the current one now?

Yes. All users who purchased the previous version will receive a discount on the next major version.
